About

General

The purpose of this app is to allow ease of communication between leaders and members of clubs. This application allows one to create events and meetings for their club and send notifications to members about upcoming ones, as well as track member attendance.

Getting Started

Once you create an account, you will be instantly connected to Clubbed In. Start by joining your clubs or starting your own!

        Help


        Create A Club

        1. Go to Your Clubs page and press Create Club
        2. Press More on the top left corner and press Create Club

        Join A Club

        1. Go to Your Clubs and press Join Club and enter Club ID
        2. Press More on the top left corner and press More and enter Club ID
        3. Search for a club and enter Club ID

        Create An Event

        Go to the desired club page, + and press New Event

        Edit Admins

        Go to the desired club page, press the gear icon and press Club Admins

        Edit News

        Go to club page and press + and press News

        Edit Information

        Go to the event or club page that you want to edit, press the gear icon, and then the Information button. You can only edit if you are the admin. At this page, the club/event can also be deleted.

        Search

        Press More on the top left corner and press Search and enter Club Name, Event Name, or School Name

        Need help with something else? Contact Us!
