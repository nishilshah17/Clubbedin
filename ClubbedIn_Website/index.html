    About Clubbed In

    What is Clubbed In?

    Clubbed In Made by students for students.

    Clubbed In is an application with the primary goal of helping high school students manage their clubs. With many features such as events and keeping track of attendance, Clubbed In is the ideal application for a highschooler to have on their smartphones. Connect and communicate with other members. The best part is...it's completely free!
